About me

A bit
about myself.

Hey, I'm David — a developer, game-maker, and musician from the Czech Republic. I started coding because I wanted to build games, and I haven't stopped since. Today my work spans web development, game engines, desktop apps, and music production.

When I'm not shipping code, I'm deep in FL Studio crafting drill beats with real guitar tracks I record myself — two guitars, a whole lot of sound design, and a dash of Linkin Park energy to tie it all together.

Everything I do is connected: I write the code, model the assets in Blender, compose the soundtrack in FL Studio, and wrap it all up into a shipped product.

The sound

My primary genre is drill — dark 808s, sliding bass, and aggressive hi-hat patterns. But I don't stop there. I layer real guitar recordings on top: a PRS Standard 24 for the aggressive stuff, a Hello Kitty Fender Squier for when things get weird.
